It’s no secret that everyone wants and expects a great user experience. This leads to lower training costs, fewer errors, and happier users. SAP Fiori provides a great user experience (UX) for SAP software. It comprises hundreds of intuitive apps as well as a set of user experience design guidelines.This course is all about taking those SAP Fiori design guidelines and showing you how to apply them to your own unique business needs. Or, put another way, building your own SAP Fiori app that’s just as delightful and user-friendly as any of the hundreds SAP has built directly.The goal of this course is to introduce you to the tools, methodologies, and best practices for designing, creating, and enhancing your very own SAP Fiori app. Included is a “design and build” challenge where you can submit your work for a chance to win a MacBook Air!
