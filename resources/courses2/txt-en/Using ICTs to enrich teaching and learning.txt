Using ICTs to enrich teaching and learning' will guide your learning in an interactive, applied manager to answer 3 questions: what are ICT's (module 1), why use ICTs in teaching and learning (module 2) and how to use ICTs to support teaching and learning (module 3 and 4). Based on your unique learning expectations and needs, you'll choose from a wide variety of learning expectations and needs, you'll chose from a wide variety of learning activities designed to help you extend, apply and use your learning from this MOOC.
