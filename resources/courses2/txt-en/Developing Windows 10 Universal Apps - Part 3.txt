Computer Science Use C# and XAML to develop Windows 10 Universal Windows Platform apps that you will be proud to publish to the new Windows store. In part three of this three-part programming series (three x 4-week courses) you develop an app that connects to the cloud for data and services, implements notifications, geofencing, and live tiles, and includes advanced features such as speech-to-text and inking. Put your existing C#/XAML skills to work creating apps for the new Windows app store. *IMPORTANT: This course will NOT teach C#, XAML or programming basics. Prior experience programming in C# and XAML is required.
