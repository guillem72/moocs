This course is the best way to create a real portfolio while getting started as an engineer. Working with a professional frontend developer you'll build interactive websites – including an online resume, a clone of Google's homepage and an interactive shopping list – using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery. Don't know what those are? Don't worry, your mentor will teach you 1-on-1. You'll master these fundamentals with 1-on-1 guidance from your mentor who is a professional developer. Your mentor will be there to help you when you get stuck, motivate you to finish a challenging project, and keep you on track. You’ll graduate with a full portfolio and a fully-stacked GitHub profile. Even if you’ve literally never coded a single line you can take the course and become a beginner frontend developer.
