Antares Auto-Tune Evo is a third-party plug-in processor that automates the correction of pitch and intonation, saving hours of time in the vocal booth. In _Pro Tools Projects: Pitch Correction with Antares Auto-Tune Evo _, author Brian Lee White explains the fundamentals of working with vocal tracks and how to correct common pitch and intonation errors using Auto-Tune. This course covers a variety of workflows and different pitch correction strategies in Auto-Tune, working in both automatic and graphical tuning modes. Exercise files are included with the course.
