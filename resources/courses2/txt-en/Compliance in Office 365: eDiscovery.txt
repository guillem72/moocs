Computer Science In Compliance in Office 365: eDiscovery, you will examine how to plan, implement, and manage eDiscovery. As organizational data stored in email and documents continues to grow, Office 365 makes it easy to find the relevant information as you need it. This training covers all the steps to effectively plan and manage discovery requests. This is the first in a series of courses concerning Compliance in Office 365.
